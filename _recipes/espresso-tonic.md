title: "Espresso Tonic"
time: "5 minutes"
servings: 1
---

**Ingredients**
- 150 ml tonic water
- Ice
- 1 shot espresso
- Citrus peel (optional)

**Method**
1. Fill a glass with ice, add tonic.
2. Gently pour hot espresso over the back of a spoon.
3. Express citrus peel over the top and drop it in.
